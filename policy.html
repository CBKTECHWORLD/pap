Information We Collect

We collect two types of data: Personally Identifiable Information (PII) and Non-Personally Identifiable Information (NPII).

PII includes your full name, contact number, email address, postal address, date of birth, gender, PAN (for tax deduction certificates), payment details (credit/debit card, UPI, net banking), and spiritual interests (e.g., deity preferences or areas of spiritual focus).

Submission of date of birth, gender, and spiritual interests is collected from all devotees and donors, as this helps us tailor spiritual communication, offerings, and community experiences. This information is collected directly from you when you donate, register for an event, or communicate with the Trust.

NPII refers to data such as your IP address, device type, browser details, and cookies that we collect automatically through website analytics tools. These help us understand visitor behaviour, ensure security, and optimise website functionality.

We collect your personal information for several sacred and operational purposes:
